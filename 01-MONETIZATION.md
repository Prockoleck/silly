# Monetization Strategy

## Philosophy
All games are **100% free, no paywalls, no gated content**. Monetization is peripheral — users come for the fun, not the store. This builds trust, traffic, and viral sharing.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Donations / Buy Me a Coffee

| Setup | Details |
|---|---|
| Platform | Ko-fi (0% commission, PayPal) |
| Placement | Subtle footer link + optional popup after 3rd game played |
| Target | $50–200/mo at 10k visitors |
| Incentive | "Support future games" — no rewards needed |

### 2. Newsletter (→ Sponsored Later)

| Setup | Details |
|---|---|
| Platform | Buttondown (free up to 1k subs) or Mailchimp (free up to 500) |
| Placement | Slide-in footer on every game page |
| Lead magnet | "Get early access to new games before anyone else" |
| Sponsor target | $200–500/mo per sponsored issue at 3k+ subscribers |

### 3. Print-on-Demand Merch

| Setup | Details |
|---|---|
| Platform | Printful + Shopify / Ko-fi shop |
| Products | T-shirts, stickers, mugs with game-related designs |
| Launch | After 3+ popular games exist (Month 4+) |
| Margin | ~30–50% per item, ~$100–400/mo |

### 4. Non-Intrusive Ads

| Setup | Details |
|---|---|
| Network | Carbon Ads / EthicalAds (curated, no trackers) |
| Format | Single static banner in sidebar or below game |
| Requirements | ~50k+ monthly pageviews to apply |
| Target | $300–1,000/mo |

### 5. Sponsorships & Brand Deals

| Setup | Details |
|---|---|
| Type | "Game sponsored by..." — custom integration |
| Reach needed | 100k+ monthly visits |
| Rate | $500–3,000 per deal |
| Channels | Email inbound, newsletter intro |

---

## Revenue Projection

| Milestone | Monthly Visits | Monthly Revenue |
|---|---|---|
| Launch (Month 1) | 1k | $0 |
| Growth (Month 3) | 10k | $50–200 |
| Traction (Month 6) | 50k | $500–1,500 |
| Sustainable (Month 9–12) | 100k | $2,000–5,000 |

**Breakdown at 100k visits/mo:**
- Donations: $200
- Newsletter sponsors: $500
- Merch: $300
- Display ads: $800
- Brand deals (1–2/quarter): $500–1,500/mo avg

---

## Key Rules

- **Never** run pop-up ads, video ads, or malware-y networks
- **Never** put a paywall on a game
- **Always** disclose sponsored content
- **Reinvest** 20% of revenue into server/domain/design assets
